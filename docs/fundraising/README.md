### Q: What's the token utility?
**A**: SEL is used for gas fees (burn mechanism), staking (10% APY), governance (voting), DeFi collateral, and validator bonds (10M SEL minimum).

### Q: When will tokens be liquid?
**A**: TGE at Month 8-10 post-Seed (mainnet launch). Initial DEX listing on Uniswap + Selendra Swap. CEX listings Month 6-12 post-TGE.

### Q: How does AI-augmented team work?
**A**: 10 specialized AI agents (Claude Code) generate 70% of code. 4 humans review and handle strategy. Proven: We shipped testnet with $3K budget.

### Q: What's the competition?
**A**: Ethereum (too slow), Solana (downtime risk), Polygon (centralized), Polkadot parachains (slot auction costs). We offer speed + security + EVM compatibility with no compromises.

### Q: What's the biggest risk?
**A**: Execution. Technology works (testnet proves it), but scaling team, growing ecosystem, and competing for developer mindshare is challenging. Mitigation: AI agents, strong advisors, focused go-to-market.
