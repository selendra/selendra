### Market Risks
- **Bear market**: Focus on product, delay token launch

- **Technical delays**: AI agents reduce risk, but still buffer timeline
- **Security issues**: External audit before mainnet ($50K reserved)
- **Team scaling**: Hire slow, focus on quality

### Fundraising Risks
- **Valuation pressure**: Have range ($5M-$8M), be flexible
- **Investor ghosting**: Cast wide net (100+ meetings)
